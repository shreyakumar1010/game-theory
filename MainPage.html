Game Theory: The Science of Strategy

So what is Game Theory?

Game Theory, to put it simply, is the study of competition and co-operation between two rational entities.
Usually, the "game" that is played is not meant in the recreational sense but a game is any interactive situation
with certain rules and restraints.

What are it's applications?

There have been a lot of developments in the field of Game Theory even though it is a young field. It has applications
in Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, Biology and many other fields.
