About

What is game theory?

It is the science of strategy. It is study of the levels of competition and co-operation between two rational entities.

What is a "game"?

Usually, the "game" that is played is not meant in the recreational sense but a game is any interactive situation with certain rules and restraints

Why is it important?

Although it is a relatively new field of study, it can be used to solve resource-allocation ad algorithm-based problems in computer science,

Types Of Games

There are several restrictions and conditions that we can impose on a situation to create different types of "Games". As it is in real life, games can become very complex and nuanced.
